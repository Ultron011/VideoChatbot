# Who Should Consider IVF

IVF is recommended for:

- Blocked or damaged fallopian tubes.
- Severe male factor infertility.
- Unexplained infertility after other treatments have failed.
- Endometriosis.
- Ovulation disorders like PCOS that haven't responded to simpler treatments.
- Women over thirty-five who want to maximize their chances.

# Common Investigations Before IVF

- **For the Female Partner**: Hormone tests on day two or three of the cycle (FSH, LH, and AMH), antral follicle count scan, uterine cavity check, thyroid, and prolactin levels.
- **For the Male Partner**: Semen analysis.
