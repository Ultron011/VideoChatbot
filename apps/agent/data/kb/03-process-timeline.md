# IVF Process & Timeline

A standard IVF cycle takes four to six weeks from start to finish:

1. **Initial consultation & baseline tests**.
2. **Ovarian stimulation** with daily injections for ten to fourteen days.
3. **Monitoring** with scans every two to three days.
4. **Egg retrieval** performed under sedation (painless).
5. **Fertilization** in the lab.
6. **Embryo transfer** into the uterus.
7. **Pregnancy test** done fourteen days after the transfer.
